Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

My Church respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

Data Purpose
Name User identification within the church system.
Phone Number (if provided) Communication between church administrators and members.
Profile Photo (optional) User profile personalization.
Church Membership Information Managing members, classes, meetings, and church activities.
Device Information Improving app performance and troubleshooting.

Permissions Used

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet – Required to communicate with our servers.
  • Notifications – To send important announcements and reminders.
  • Camera (Optional) – To upload or update profile pictures.
  • Photo Library / Storage (Optional) – To select profile images.

Permissions are only used for their intended functionality and can be denied by the user where supported by the operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Authenticate users securely.
  • Manage church members and meetings.
  • Provide church announcements and notifications.
  • Improve application performance and reliability.
  • Provide technical support when needed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information may only be shared:

  • With authorized church administrators who manage your church.
  • When required by law.
  • To trusted service providers that help operate the application (such as secure cloud hosting).

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention

Your information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the application's services or as required by applicable laws.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account from the Profile screen in the application. Detailed instructions are available on the public Account Deletion page.

Deletion removes your login account, credentials, roles, claims, authentication records, contact details, registration data, profile photo, servant profile, classroom assignments, and personal custom-field values. Issued authentication tokens are rejected after the account is removed.

Shared church operational records may remain where deleting them would affect other users, but references to the deleted account are removed or anonymized. Minimal information may also be retained where required for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or enforcement of agreements.

Encrypted disaster-recovery backups may contain deleted information for up to 30 days. Backups are isolated from normal application use and are removed through the regular backup rotation. If a backup is restored, deletion requests are reapplied.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account where permitted.
  • Request removal of your personal information.

Children's Privacy

The application is intended for church management and member communication. It is not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children without appropriate authorization from their parent, guardian, or church administrator where applicable.

  • Google Play Services

These providers maintain their own privacy policies regarding the information they process.
