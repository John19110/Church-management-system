Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

My Church respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we use it, how long we keep it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

  • Name, phone number, authentication credentials, and account role.
  • Optional profile photo, birth date, and church joining date.
  • Church, meeting, classroom, membership, attendance, and custom-field information.
  • Limited device and diagnostic information needed for security and reliability.

How we use information

We use this information to create and authenticate accounts, provide church-management features, manage approvals and assignments, communicate with users, protect the service, troubleshoot problems, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only with authorized church administrators, service providers operating the application, or authorities when required by law.

Security and retention

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. Active account data is kept while needed to provide the service. Legal, fraud-prevention, security, and dispute records may be retained for the period required by law or legitimate operational need.

Account deletion

You can permanently delete your account from the Profile screen in the app, or follow the instructions on our Account Deletion page.

Deletion removes your Identity account, credentials, authentication records, roles, claims, tokens, contact details, registration data, profile photo, servant profile, assignments, and personal custom-field values. Your issued JWT is rejected after the account is removed.

Shared operational records may remain where deletion would affect other users, but references to your deleted account are removed or anonymized. Minimal information may remain where required for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

Encrypted disaster-recovery backups may contain deleted information for up to 30 days. Backups are isolated from normal use and deleted through regular rotation. If restored for disaster recovery, deletion requests are reapplied.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. You can revoke optional device permissions through your operating-system settings.

Children’s privacy

The service is intended for church administration and does not knowingly collect information directly from children without appropriate parent, guardian, or church authorization.
