Delete your account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete from the app

  1. Sign in to My Church.
  2. Open Profile and scroll to the bottom.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Review the warning and confirm Delete Account again.

Your request is normally completed immediately. In unusual cases involving backups, full removal may take up to 30 days.

If you cannot access the app

Email johnpolis122@gmail.com from the phone number or email associated with your account. We may ask you to verify ownership before processing the request.

Data permanently deleted

  • Your login account, credentials, roles, claims, tokens, and authentication records.
  • Your name, phone number, profile photo, birth/joining dates, and pending-registration data.
  • Your servant profile, classroom assignments, and personal custom-field values.
  • Locally stored authentication tokens, preferences, and cached user data on the device used to delete the account.

Data that may be retained

Shared church operational records may remain where deleting them would affect other users—for example attendance events or shared field definitions—but they are detached from and no longer identify your deleted account. Minimal records may be retained when required for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or enforcement of agreements.

Encrypted disaster-recovery backups may retain deleted data for up to 30 days. Backup copies are isolated from normal application use and are removed through the regular backup rotation.
